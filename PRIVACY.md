# Privacy Policy for Decenza DE1

**Last updated: December 26, 2025**

## Overview

Decenza DE1 is an open-source application for controlling Decent Espresso DE1 machines. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The app operates entirely on your device and communicates only with:
- Your DE1 espresso machine (via Bluetooth)
- Your Bluetooth scale (if connected)
- Visualizer.coffee (only if you enable this feature and provide your credentials)

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

- **Bluetooth** - Required to communicate with your DE1 machine and Bluetooth scales
- **Location** - Required by Android for Bluetooth Low Energy scanning (we do not track or store your location)
- **Internet** - Only used for optional Visualizer.coffee uploads and screensaver video downloads

## Data Storage

All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to us or any third party (except Visualizer.coffee if you choose to enable that feature).

## Third-Party Services

**Visualizer.coffee** (optional) - If you enable shot uploads, your shot data is sent to visualizer.coffee using credentials you provide. Please refer to their privacy policy for how they handle your data.

## Open Source

Decenza DE1 is open source. You can review the complete source code at:
https://github.com/Kulitorum/de1-qt

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on GitHub:
